Welcome to Delhizones.com. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. For further information, please consult Google's Privacy & Terms here.

Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device by websites that you visit. We use cookies to help us analyze our web traffic, to serve advertisements, and to improve the functionality of our website. You can choose to disable cookies through your individual browser options.

Data Security

We have put in place appropriate security measures to prevent your personal data from being accidentally lost, used or accessed in an unauthorised way. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. While our EDU section provides tools for learning, they are intended for use with parental guidance.
